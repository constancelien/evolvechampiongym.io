Who are we ?

Being the #1 Martial Arts Organization in Asia, Evolve Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) is known for its state-of-the-art amenities and its world-renowned training programs crafted and taught by World Champions from all over the world.
The gym has also produced many local champions of their own, making each athlete a trailblazer in their respective martial arts in Singapore.



  1. Evolve has the most number of World Champions available anywhere in the world.


  2. The world-renowned Evolve Instructor Team is the largest and most experienced team in Asia.


  3. The Evolve Instructor Team has over 1,000 years of world championship experience.


  4. With 800+ classes per week, Evolve has the largest and most convenient class schedules in Asia.


  5. The award-winning Evolve academies are state-of-the-art, modern 5 star facilities.


Our Mission

To unleash greatness in everyone through authentic martial arts under World Champions.
To create a safe, fun, and supportive environment that enables everyone to enjoy the benefits of martial arts.
Such as fitness, good health, confidence, mental grit, discipline, self-defense and lifelong friendships.
